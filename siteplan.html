Site name

Dry Oar River Rafting - A historical river adventure.

Site Purpose

The primary purpose of the website is to sell river rafting trips. The secondary purpose is informing visitors about the joys of rafting adventures and the histories of the rivers we offer rafting trips on. I want to create a company that uses the river rafting trip as a way to connect to the history of an area rather then just another trip. While everything in the website will be pulling the consumer to the 'book an adventure' page, the focus will seem to be on giving the consumer information on rafting, the history of the river, and the experiences of the natives of the land.

Target Audience

There is 2 target audeinces here. The first is families looking for unique experiences to introduce their children to. The second audience is history/nature buffs. Those looking to connect to the history of an area and the native people that used to live there.
